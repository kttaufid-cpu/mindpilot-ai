“Create a next-generation AI Personal Assistant app called MindPilot AI, fully powered by artificial intelligence without any human backend. This app is designed for users aged 25–50 who want a smarter way to manage daily life, finances, tasks, routines, and personal productivity. The app must include two versions: FREE (lifetime access) and PREMIUM (RM9.90/month with a 7-day free trial).”

1. BUSINESS MODEL

FREE VERSION (Lifetime Access):

Lifetime access with limitations.

Ads (minimal & non-intrusive).

Limited AI responses per day (10–15).

Limited storage.

No financial sync.

No advanced automation.

Basic summaries only.

PREMIUM VERSION (RM9.90/month):

7-day free trial.

Unlimited AI responses.

Unlimited cloud storage.

Full automated task management.

Smart Financial Sync (banks, e-wallets, bills).

Advanced daily insights & personalized routines.

Priority AI processing.

No ads.

Encrypted cloud backup.

2. CORE FEATURES

AI Task Manager & Automation

Free: manual tasks + simple reminders.

Premium: auto-generated daily tasks, priority sorting, location-based reminders.

AI Personal Finance Manager

Free: manual tracking + basic stats.

Premium: auto categorization, spending insights, bill alerts, financial forecasting.

AI Life Organizer

Free: up to 10 documents.

Premium: unlimited storage + automatic AI sorting.

AI Wellness & Health Coach

Free: mood tracking only.

Premium: personalized routines, wellness insights, habit tracking automation.

AI Personal Coach

Free: up to 3 goals/month.

Premium: unlimited goals + monthly action plans.

Smart Communication Assistant

Free: 5 summaries/day.

Premium: unlimited summaries + voice mode + instant message digest.

3. UI/UX REQUIREMENTS

Minimalist, premium feel.

Smooth navigation with 3–4 main tabs.

Color theme: cobalt/teal/dark mode friendly.

Everything must "feel intelligent".

4. AI ENGINE REQUIREMENTS

100% AI-driven automation.

Learns user habits, preferences, routines.

Predictive behaviour (suggest tasks, warn financial shifts, plan days automatically).

Human-like tone and fast response.

5. OUTPUT REQUIRED

The system must generate:

Full app structure

Free vs Premium feature map

User journey (onboarding → daily use)

Monetization flow & upgrade triggers

Branding, tagline, and positioning

UI wireframe concepts

Data model overview

Privacy & security plan

The final result must position this as an all-in-one AI life assistant: tasks, finance, routines, wellness, document management, and personal coaching — fully automated and personalized.”
